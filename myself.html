About

At the start of 2019, I was introduced to the skill known as 'programming' and I really enjoyed the problem solving aspect of a code. It was as if mathematics and programming was cut from the same cloth, so I was able to quickly comprehend basic programming techniques with the help of a mentor. I started by indulging myself in online Capture-The-Flag(CTF) challenges and built my knowledge by reading write-ups or watching youtube on how experts solve computer security problems. I also looked out for cybersecurity programmes that could teach me more about the course.

My participation in the Youth-Cyber-Exploration-Programme during my mid-year breaks in 2019 was the fuel to spark my passion for CyberSecurity. Throughout the program, I found myself having tons of fun picking up skills and achieving Top 10 Winner Award along with 2nd Runner-Up in the CTF competition with my teammates played a huge role in my resolution as it highlighted my ability to pursue this career.

Despite having to fix millions of bugs that would regularly appear on my programmes or having to crack my brains to solve CTF challenges, my passion for it only seems to get stronger every day.
